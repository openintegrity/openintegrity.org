Contribute

We invite experts which understand end-user security and privacy through a range of practices to join us and help:

  • Contribute data
  • Review and verify submitted data
  • Participate in a transparent, open and quality debate around key aspects of the Open Integrity Initiative.

Join the initiative

Impact Consultation

An ongoing Impact Consultation will focus on local organisations or intermediaries that are in contact and aware of real world impact and potential impact on users at risk.

Consulted partners will help ensure that Open Integrity is focused on how end-users are impacted by software practices in security and privacy, with a particular attention to vulnerable users.

Measurement Partnerships

Are you a researcher, a team or an organisation surveying or collecting data on security and privacy best practices? Do you use free and open source software collector agents, or open data workflows to collect qualitative data? Do you publish your data with an open data licence.

Then read on to find out more about becoming an Open Integrity Measurement Partner.
