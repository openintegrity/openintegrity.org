Pull Requests

I think pull request are in fact a very good match for a large task that cuts across several issues but has a clear end and requires participation. I think therefore it's a good fit at the design phase for instance for:

  • Getting the website's homepage language and design and development aligned and have contribution from the whole team and beyond.

Later on it could be used as an approach for for instance:

  • The monthly summary of the overal evolution of the practice space (the "Synthesis" or "Wrapping" phase of Assembl).
  • Evolution of the threat landscape and discussion of implications across practices.
  • Within specific roles
  • Dispute on the data platform? This would mean a deep integration of the data platform with Git/Gitlab as a workflow management component which might be a good thing
  • Qualitative reviews on the data platform? For instance the TOSDR workflow could probably be moved to there (from the current google group workflow)
  • Moderation of impact submission?

Could be used for:

  • a particular event where information needs to be processed
  • a document that's parsed or another
