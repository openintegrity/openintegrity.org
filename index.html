Open Integrity is booting up.

We provide reliable and evidence based answers to questions such as:

  • Which tools can be downloaded securely and verified to be authentic?
  • Which tools provide end-to-end encryption by default?
  • Which terms of service protect your private data?

You can read more about the project.

Our Approach

Open Integrity focuses on gathering measurement and claims about the adoption of best practices that support the security and privacy of software. We're developing partnerships in order to define metrics and collect data that will be available for an audience of professionals (software engineers, trainers, advocacy organizations) and will help provide answers about best practices adoption.
