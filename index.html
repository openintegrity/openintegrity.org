The Open Integrity Initiative is booting up.

We provide reliable and evidence based answers to questions such as:

  • Which tools are open source?
  • Which provide end-to-end encryption or support two factor authentication?
  • Which can be downloaded securely and verified to be authentic?

You can read more about the project.

Our Approach

Open Integrity focuses on gathering measurement and claims about the adoption of best practices that support the security and privacy of software. We're developing partnerships in order to define metrics and collect data that will be available for an audience of professionals (software engineers, trainers, advocacy organizations) and will help provide answers about best practices adoption.

Get Involved

If you're collecting data or doing research on security and privacy then we'd like to hear about your work! We're inviting a range of experts to contribute to the design of the index and identifying best practices in the following key areas:

Technology

  • Dependency Management
  • Architecture
  • Vulnerabilities

Policy

  • Terms of Service
  • Corporate Accountability
  • Transparency reporting

Design

  • Default Settings
  • User Experience
  • Translations
